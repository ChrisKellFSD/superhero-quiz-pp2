How to Play
Welcome to the Superhero Quiz Game.
To play, click the 'Begin' button and choose which theme you would like the questions to be on, Marvel or DC.
Each quiz has 10 questions with 4 possible answers to choose from. Once complete, click the 'Submit Quiz' to see your score. Each quiz can then be replayed again. Good luck!
